# Open Sheets

**Free Excel spreadsheet templates and plain-English tax guides for UK small businesses.**

🇬🇧 Built for UK tax rules. No subscriptions. No signup. No catch.

## What Is Open Sheets?

Open Sheets is the UK's answer to Vertex42 — a free collection of professional Excel templates designed specifically for UK small business owners, sole traders, landlords, and freelancers.

Every template is:
- ✅ **Free** — no paywall, no trial, no credit card
- ✅ **UK-focused** — HMRC expense categories, UK tax rates, GBP
- ✅ **Simple** — only blue cells need your input
- ✅ **Compatible** — works in Excel, Google Sheets, and LibreOffice
- ✅ **No email gate** — download instantly
