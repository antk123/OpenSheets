# OpenSheets UK Template Research — 100 Templates

## Competitor Analysis Summary

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Pricing Model |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Vertex42** | Massive library, well-documented, trusted since 2003 | US-focused, limited UK HMRC specifics | Free + premium packs (one-time) |
| **TemplateLab** | Legal/business forms, high SEO | Spreadsheet quality variable, US legal docs | Free (ad-supported) |
| **Spreadsheet123** | SMB-focused, clear licensing | Smaller library, dated designs | Free + affordable paid |
| **Someka** | Polished dashboards, professional | Premium pricing, not UK-specific | Premium one-time |
| **Indzara** | Industry-focused, good guides | Limited UK compliance features | One-time |
| **PayToolkit** | UK CIS focused, HMRC rates | Niche (construction only) | Free |
| **EasyMTD** | MTD VAT compliance | Limited template variety | Free |
| **Microsoft Create** | Official, high compatibility | Generic, not UK-localised | Free + M365 |

**OpenSheets Opportunity:** UK-first, HMRC-compliant, free, no email gate. Own the "UK Vertex42" position.
